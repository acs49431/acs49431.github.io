The Dragon

The Dragon Nikkia lost both her parents in the great dragon eradication of the late Bronze Age. She found Kaya when she was only a 15 year old dragonkin and the two have been closely bonded ever since. She is an intellegent beast who has a tendency to eat people who bother her, makin her a perfect companion for Kaya. Together, she hopes to work with Kaya to become one of the greatest human/dragon parings of the Age.
