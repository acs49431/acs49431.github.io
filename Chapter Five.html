Chapter FIVE

The End

The shimmering scales of the great dragon flickered and faded into the blackness of the starry night. Looking up, Alex could almost see the golden haired rider swaying up and down on crook of the dragon’s back, though it wasn’t long before the white scales and long blonde hair had vanished from view completely. Prince Alex turned to Sir David.

Do you think she will be happy as a rider?

Absolutely. She has the fire seen only in the greatest of knights, Sir David replied. But you, your highness. What will you do? She was your chance for a crown.

Alex smiled.

That’s alright. I don’t think it would have worked between us anyway. I believe I have already found a better match, Alex nudged Sir David’s shoulder and ran cackling into his castle home, leaving Sir David with red cheeks and a befuddled expression on his face.
