The Prince

Prince Alex, know for being kind of a know it all, successfully conviced his brother to give him the last cookie when he was only four years old. When he was eleven he was given his very own pony and when he was thirteen he had his first love. By the time of the story, Alex was a seventeen year old mastermind who everyone was secretly jealous of.
