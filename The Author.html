About the Author

Ashton Sanders is a know book enthusiast. She is also considered a semi-professional dog petter, place 4th out of 3 in a national hopscotch competition, and can almost always boil water without setting the house on fire. Her favorite pasttimes include but are not limited to eating, sleeping, practicing satanic rituals, and playing soccer. She has two fiends, only one of whom is imaginary, and has a tendency to gossip with her pet stuffed animal Mr. Noodle when things get heated. Overall she considers herself passible as a sub-par human being whose only real ambition in life is to be a llama.
